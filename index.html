Johnson Neil Hanma

A very talented, kind-hearted and intelligent student

The most dedicated Infirmarian of 2019/2020 academic session.

Here's a time line of Johnson Neil Hanma's life



  • 2002 - Born in Benin City, Edo State. Nigeria

  • 2014 - Gains Admission into Immaculate Conception College as the student with the best grades in the school's entrance exam

  • 2015 - is diagnosed of Asthma

  • 2016 - is selected for the Nigerian national Mathematics competetion and he suceeds as the best Mathematics student for that academic year. He also suffered a chronic asthma attack that same year that left him in comma for 2 weeks.

  • 2017 - was awarded the best student in sciences for Senior Secondary School. He then suffered another tragic asthma attack but He recovered quite well.

  • 2018 - Is appointed as the school's assistant Safety Prefect for the 2018/2019 academic session. He also wins an award for best in Mathematics for that academic year.

  • 2019 - is appointed as the school's Infirmarian for the 2019/2020 academic session. He becomes a very kind hearted Infirmarian, he shows love to the ill students and is very compassionate towards junior students in the school.

  • 2020 - He dies of a serious asthma attack due to unavailability of his personal inhailer. He is rushed to the nearest hospital but he couldn't recover. He dies two days after the asthma attack but he was opportuned to receive the sacrament of annointing of the sick and the sacrament of confession. He died a peaceful death. His last words were the prayers found under Christ's Sepulcher.


Johnson Neil Hanma life is indeed the life of a dedicated student, all all-round scholar, a pious and neat student. Never will i forget what a great student he was, I will forever remember him in my heart and continue to pray for the peaceful repose of his soul. Eternal rest grant unto Johnson Neil Hanma, O Lord, and let your perpertual light shine upon him.

-- Immaculate Conception College Principal, Father Anthony Uzumaki.
